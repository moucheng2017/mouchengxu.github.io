Hey there, my name is Xu Mou-Cheng (徐 谋 成). I'm a PhD student at Centre for Medical Image Computing (CMIC) at University College London (UCL), advised by Joseph Jacob , Daniel Alexander , Fred Wilson and Neil Oxtoby. I am very grateful for the funding sponsored by GlaxoSmithKline (GSK) and UCL Engineering Dean's prize.

My research interests are deep learning, computer vision and applications in healthcare and other domains:

  • Representation learning: Semi-supervised, self-supervised, one-shot
  • Uncertainty
  • Interpretability: counterfactural inferences, salience visulisation
  • Temporal data mining: video segmentation, disease progression modelling
